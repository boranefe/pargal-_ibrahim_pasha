THE SILENT EXECUTION

March 15. Invited to Topkapi Palace for a Ramadan dinner. He slept in the Sultan's quarters, as he had for years.

In the morning, his body was found with marks of strangulation. He was buried in an unmarked grave in Galata, denying him a state funeral.
